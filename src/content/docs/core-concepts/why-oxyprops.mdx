## Design Tokens focused

At OxyProps, we understand the importance of design consistency and efficiency. That's why we built OxyProps with a strong focus on design tokens. With OxyProps, you can easily define and manage your design system's colors, typography, spacing, and more, using CSS custom properties. By centralizing your design tokens, you can achieve a consistent look and feel across your entire website or application, making it easier to maintain and update your styles.

## User First

OxyProps puts the user experience at the forefront. We believe that designing and developing for the web should be a seamless and intuitive process. OxyProps integrates directly into popular page builders, empowering you to harness the full potential of CSS custom properties and custom elements without leaving your familiar design environment. This user-centric approach allows you to focus on creating remarkable websites that deliver an exceptional experience to your audience.

## Fast by Default

Performance is a top priority in today's web landscape. OxyProps is designed to be fast by default, ensuring your website loads quickly and efficiently. By leveraging the power of CSS custom properties, OxyProps reduces the need for repetitive and bloated CSS code. This streamlined approach optimizes your website's performance, resulting in faster load times and improved user engagement. Say goodbye to unnecessary overhead and hello to a lightning-fast website.

## Easy to Use

We believe that powerful tools should also be accessible to all. OxyProps is designed with simplicity in mind, making it easy for both beginners and experienced developers to use. The plugin seamlessly integrates with your existing workflow, allowing you to leverage the benefits of CSS custom properties without a steep learning curve. With clear documentation and intuitive controls, OxyProps empowers you to create stunning designs with ease, saving you time and effort in the process.

## Fully featured, but Flexible

OxyProps offers a comprehensive set of features to cater to your diverse design needs. Whether you're working on a small personal project or a large-scale team collaboration, OxyProps provides the tools and flexibility to adapt to your requirements. Customize and extend your design system with ease, thanks to the plugin's extensive capabilities. OxyProps offers a balance between being fully featured and adaptable, ensuring it can meet the demands of any project, big or small.
